About Outsourcing

What is outsourcing for us?

For us, outsourcing is a practice of helping a business by performing services or creating goods for them that were traditionally being done by their staff so that they can reduce costs by 50%-70%!

Benefits of outsourcing

1.)Reduced labour cost, no medical coverage and saving on office space and office insurance.
2.)Works round the clock 24/7.
3.)Increased efficiency.
4.)Better management and you can focus on other important tasks.
5.)Reduced risk.
6.)Access to skilled employees and best resources.

Outsourcing with a social cause

We believe in giving back to the community so 10% of the profit will go to charities that will give hope to those who are in greatest need.
